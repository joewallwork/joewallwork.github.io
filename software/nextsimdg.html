nextSIM-DG¶

nextSIM-DG is a next-generation discontinuous Galerkin sea ice model written in C++.

I was actively involved in nextSIM-DG development during a project as part of my work as a Research Software Engineer at the Institute of Computing for Climate Science (ICCS).
